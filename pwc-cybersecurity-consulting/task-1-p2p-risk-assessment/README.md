# Task 1 – P2P Risk Assessment

## Objective

Perform a risk assessment of the Procure-to-Pay (P2P) process and identify business process and IT control gaps.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Risk Assessment
- Internal Controls
- IT General Controls (ITGC)
- Segregation of Duties
- Access Control Review
- SOX-Oriented Control Analysis

## Deliverables

- Findings Summary
- Business Process Gaps
- IT Control Gaps
- Follow-Up Questions
