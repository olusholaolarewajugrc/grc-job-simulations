# Resource Notes

## Concepts Learned

### Procure-to-Pay (P2P)

The Procure-to-Pay process governs purchasing activities from requisition through payment.

### Three-Way Match

A payment should only occur when:

- Purchase Order
- Goods Receipt
- Vendor Invoice

all agree.

### Segregation of Duties (SoD)

Critical financial activities should be divided among multiple individuals to reduce fraud risk.

### SOX Relevance

SOX emphasizes effective internal controls over financial reporting and access management.

## Key Takeaways

- Business process controls are as important as technical controls.
- Weak access management can become a financial reporting risk.
- Audit findings should always connect a control gap to a business risk.
